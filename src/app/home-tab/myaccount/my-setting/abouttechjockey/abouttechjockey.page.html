About Techjockey

Techjockey.com is an e-commerce platform, selling a wide range of software solutions to startups, SMEs, MSMEs, corporates, and even individuals. It caters to the demand for IT solutions across industries like Retail, e-Commerce, ITES, Hospitality, Healthcare, Education; and business functions such as HR, Sales, Marketing, Legal and many more.

At Techjockey, business customers can purchase software products online by using various payments methods such as mobile wallets, Net Banking, Debit/Card, EMI and Credit. Besides, the platform simplifies the entire process of finding the right software solution for businesses. Our sales experts also consult business customers and understand their requirements to help them make the best software purchase.

Techjockey was co-founded by serial entrepreneurs Akash Nangia& Arjun Mittal in 2016 with a mission to aggregate small and large software vendors and fulfill the software needs of SMEs, especially located in tier 2 and tier 3 cities. The core business model of Techjockey involves the listing of unrepresented software solutions from premium and niche vendors and matching them with industry-specific needs.

The company helps original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) by giving their software the digital presence and global exposure. At present, Techjockey enjoys premium partnership with IT giants like VMWare, Dell and Microsoft, and tech start-ups alike.
